Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Type Approval process take?
On average, the process takes between 2 to 4 working weeks, assuming all technical documents are complete and meet the MTIT standards.
Is an in-country local representative mandatory?
Yes, according to Yemeni regulations, foreign manufacturers must appoint a licensed local agent like Continental to handle all administrative and technical procedures.
What is the validity of the Type Approval certificate?
Certificates are generally valid for 2 years. We provide renewal services 3 months prior to the expiration date.
Are test samples required for every application?
In most cases, technical test reports from accredited international laboratories (ILAC/MRA) are sufficient, but the Ministry reserves the right to request a physical sample for certain technologies.
